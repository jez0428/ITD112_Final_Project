Location of World Cities

World cities, also known as global cities or metropolises, are major urban centers that play a crucial role in the global economic, cultural, and political landscape. These cities serve as key hubs for commerce, finance, innovation, and cultural exchange, exerting significant influence on a regional, national, and international scale. They attract people from various backgrounds, offer diverse opportunities, and contribute to shaping global trends.
